# 🎯 Executive Summary

Every year, millions of students and professionals invest significant time, money, and effort learning new skills.

Yet most career decisions are still based on assumptions rather than data.

Questions such as:

* Which skills are actually in demand?
* Am I qualified for my target role?
* What should I learn next?
* Which skill will increase my salary potential the most?
* How does my profile compare to current market expectations?

often remain unanswered.

SkillGap AI was built to solve this problem.

By combining job market intelligence, analytics, AI-driven recommendations, and career planning frameworks, the platform converts raw labor market data into actionable career insights.

Instead of randomly choosing courses, technologies, or certifications, users receive a personalized roadmap based on market demand and career goals.

# 🌟 Platform Overview

SkillGap AI functions as a personal career strategist.

Users provide:

* Current Skills
* Experience Level
* Education Background
* Target Role

The platform then:

1. Analyzes market demand
2. Compares candidate profile with industry requirements
3. Identifies missing competencies
4. Prioritizes learning opportunities
5. Generates AI-powered recommendations
6. Creates structured learning plans

# 🤖 AI Scoring Methodology

Career Match Score is calculated using multiple dimensions.

```text
Career Match Score

=
(
Skills Match × 40%
+
Experience Alignment × 25%
+
Market Relevance × 20%
+
Education Fit × 15%
)
```

### Score Interpretation

| Score    | Meaning                       |
| -------- | ----------------------------- |
| 90–100   | Excellent Match               |
| 75–89    | Strong Candidate              |
| 60–74    | Moderate Gap                  |
| Below 60 | Significant Upskilling Needed |
